About Us

Incandescence is the annual cultural festival of NIT Silchar , bringing together students from across the country to celebrate creativity, talent, and culture. The fest features a diverse range of events, from dance & music to literary and gaming competitions, making it a perfect blend of fun and competition.

About NIT Silchar

NIT Silchar, one of India's premier technical institutes, is known for its academic excellence and cultural diversity. Established in 1967, it has been a hub of innovation, research, and extracurricular activities. With a vibrant student community, the institute fosters an environment where learning and creativity go hand in hand.

Dance

The Art of Dance

Dance is a universal language of expression that connects people across cultures. It embodies rhythm, emotion, and storytelling. In a cultural fest, dance performances can showcase diversity and creativity, bringing together traditional and contemporary styles.
